# 🤖 From Curious to Capable: An AI Course for Young Minds

### A complete, hands-on journey from "What is AI?" to building your own intelligent projects

**Designed for ages 11–14 · No prior coding needed · Mostly free tools · Roughly a 3–6 month adventure**

---

## 📖 How to Use This Course

This course is built like a video game: you start at Level 1 and unlock harder, cooler stuff as you go. Each **Module** is a world. Each **Lesson** is a quest. Most lessons have three parts:

- **🧠 The Big Idea** — what you're learning and why it matters
- **🎮 Do It** — a hands-on activity (some need a computer, some don't)
- **💬 Think About It** — questions to chew on (great to discuss with a parent)

**Pacing suggestion:** One lesson per sitting (about 30–60 minutes), two or three sittings a week. Don't rush. The "wow" moments come from *doing*, not just reading. Take a weekend for each bigger project.

> **📋 A note for parents** is included at the start of every module. You don't need to know AI yourself — the course is written so you can learn alongside your child. Your main job is curiosity, encouragement, and a bit of supervision around the online tools.
