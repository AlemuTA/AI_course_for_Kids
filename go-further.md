# 🚀 Go Further — Where to Head Next

Once the course is done, here's how to keep growing.

## Free tools you already met (bookmark these)
- **Google Quick Draw** — *quickdraw.withgoogle.com* (fun, gentle warm-up showing AI guessing your doodles)
- **Teachable Machine** — *teachablemachine.withgoogle.com*
- **Machine Learning for Kids** — *machinelearningforkids.co.uk*
- **TensorFlow Playground** — *playground.tensorflow.org*
- **MIT App Inventor** — *appinventor.mit.edu*
- **Code.org's AI lessons** — structured, school-aligned activities

## Skills to deepen
- Keep going with **Python** — it unlocks everything in real AI.
- Try **Scratch** game projects to strengthen coding logic.
- Explore **Khan Academy** for the math (algebra, statistics) that powers AI later on.

## Mindset for the journey
- Build things, break things, and stay curious.
- Always ask *"how does this actually work?"* and *"is this fair / true / safe?"*
- The people who will shape AI's future are the ones who understand it **and** care about using it well. After this course — that's you. 🌟
